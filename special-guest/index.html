What to expect

How long is the chat?
About 30 minutes. We'll hop on a video call, record it, and keep it loose — like two designers chatting over coffee.
Do I need to prepare?
Nope. Just show up with something you're excited to talk about. We'll steer the conversation, you just bring the energy.
What happens after recording?
We edit it down, post it on YouTube, and share it with the community. You'll get a link to preview before it goes live.
I'm early in my career — can I still apply?
Absolutely. We love fresh perspectives. If you've got thoughts about design and a willingness to share them, you're qualified.
